### Collection Description
Vince Lewandowski is an educator in the Pittsburgh Area with a history of work as the house manager of the downtown dance club "Pegasus" located at 818 Liberty Avenue. Vince's time at Pegasus spanned the 1980s and 90's, working both with the owners of the nightclub as well as managing the club staff and booking entertainment.
While at Pegasus, Vince passively collected media made by the performers and dj's who became institutions of their own including DJ Bill Burrs, creator of Breaktime Studios , house-music DJ Jim (aka Jimmy) Lewis - featured in memorium in the [MS89 Queen of the Megamix event with Pittsburgh vogue house mother and drag performer Milan Tre'zur.](https://pittsburghqueerhistory.com/s89-queen-of-the-megamix-with-special-guest-host-milan-trezur/)


### Series *(Items within the collection organized by topic or media)*
- [**Series 1: VHS Tapes**](#series-1-VHS-Tapes-items) - VHS Tapes kept from performances at Pegasus (818 Liberty Avenue) as well as promotional material for screen play behind the bar.
- [**Series 2: Audio Cassette Mixtapes**](#series-2-Audio-Cassette-Mixtapes-items) - Mixtapes by Pegasus DJ's including Billy Burrs and Jimmy Lewis as well as commercial tapes and complications ordered for playing at the bar.
